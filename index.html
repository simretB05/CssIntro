Looking at a bright white screen for long periods of time can cause eye strain and fatigue. This is because the intense light from the screen can cause the muscles in your eyes to work harder to focus, leading to discomfort and fatigue. Additionally, staring at a screen for extended periods can lead to dry eyes, which can cause itching, burning, and discomfort. To reduce eye strain, it is recommended to take frequent breaks, adjust screen brightness and contrast, and use anti-glare screens. It's also a good idea to blink frequently to help keep your eyes moisturized.
